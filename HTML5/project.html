Top 3 Fiction Book Series

1.Harry Potter

  1. Harry Potter and the Philosopher Stone
  2. Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets
  3. Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban
  4. Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire
  5. Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix
  6. Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince
  7. Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows

This is the most popular fictional series in the entire word. It adds up to more than 1 million words and it is great for anybody wishing to get into reading or a great magical world.

2.Percy Jackson and the Olympians

  1. Percy Jackson: The Lightning Thief
  2. Percy Jackson: The Sea of Monsters
  3. Percy Jackson: The Titan's Curse
  4. Percy Jackson: The Battle of the Labyrinth
  5. Percy Jackson: The Last Olympian

This is another popular series that has 2 movie adaptations that will look nothing like what you'd expect after reading the books. It also just got the first season of its TV series released on Disney+. This is a way shorter series than Harry Potter with a little less than 500,000 total words.

3.The Hunger Games

  1. The Hunger Games
  2. Catching Fire
  3. Mockingjay
  4. The Ballad of Songbirds and Snakes

This is a great and short series for anybody looking for a more adult and dystopian fictional book series. The fourth book is not a continuation of the other three but it can be read as a prologue to the actual story of the Hunger Games.
